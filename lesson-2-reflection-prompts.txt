How is the staging area different from the working directory and the repository?
What value do you think it offers?
	Staging area is like a temporary place where you place files you ready to commit. Working directory and repository are permanent places to hold your current working files, regardless of ready to commit or not. Staging area offers a stage where you could decide which files to commit, therefore have more control over your commit.
